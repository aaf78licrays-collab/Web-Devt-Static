Our Story

Our History

Founded in 1985, WILD AFRICA emerged from a passionate vision to protect Africa's endangered species and pristine ecosystems. For nearly four decades, we've been at the forefront of wildlife conservation, working alongside local communities to create sustainable solutions that benefit both nature and people.

Our Background

What began as a small group of conservationists in Kenya has grown into a continental force for change. We've expanded our reach across 15 African nations, establishing protected reserves, anti-poaching units, and educational programs that have helped save countless species from extinction.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to conserving Africa's most vulnerable wildlife and wild places. Through science-based approaches, community partnerships, and innovative conservation strategies, we work tirelessly to ensure that Africa's natural heritage thrives for generations to come.
