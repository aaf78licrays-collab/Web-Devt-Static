How We Work

Our multifaceted approach combines various disciplines and strategies to achieve lasting conservation impact across Africa.

Public Policy

We advocate for stronger wildlife protection laws and sustainable development policies. Our policy team works with governments to draft legislation, establish protected areas, and enforce anti-poaching regulations.

Recent achievements include helping pass stricter ivory trade bans in 8 countries and establishing 3 new national parks totaling over 2 million hectares.

Science & Research

Our research programs provide critical data that informs conservation strategies. We conduct population surveys, genetic studies, migration tracking, and ecosystem health assessments.

Our scientists publish peer-reviewed research and collaborate with universities worldwide to advance conservation science and develop innovative solutions.

Business Partnerships

We partner with responsible businesses to promote sustainable practices and eco-tourism. These partnerships generate revenue for conservation while creating economic opportunities for local communities.

Our eco-tourism initiatives have generated over $15 million annually, directly funding conservation programs and community development.

Wildlife Conservation

Direct action in the field is at the heart of our work. We deploy ranger units, operate wildlife rescue centers, implement breeding programs, and conduct habitat restoration projects.

Our field teams have rescued over 2,500 animals, trained 450 rangers, and restored more than 100,000 hectares of degraded habitat.
